Privacy Policy
We don't collect
your data. Full stop.
Effective Date: 27 April 2026

LargeNote is a local-first word processor. Everything you write, every document you create, every spreadsheet you build — it all lives on your Mac. We have no servers, no accounts, no telemetry. This policy explains that in detail.

🔒

The short version

LargeNote does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or document content whatsoever. All data resides exclusively on your local device under your full control.

📄

Information We Collect

We collect no information from you. LargeNote operates entirely offline. There are no user accounts, no registration forms, no analytics SDKs, and no background network requests. The app never contacts our servers because we have none.

📁

Your Documents & Files

All documents, spreadsheets, images, and content you create in LargeNote are stored locally on your Mac in locations you choose. We cannot access, read, or view your files in any way. Your content never leaves your device.

📡

Network & Internet Access

LargeNote does not make any network requests during normal operation. The app does not:

  • Send usage analytics or crash reports to any server
  • Download ads or tracking scripts
  • Sync your content to the cloud
  • Check for updates in the background (macOS handles this via the App Store)
  • Connect to any third-party service
🍪

Cookies & Tracking

LargeNote uses no cookies, pixels, fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology. There is nothing to track — the app has no web backend or user identity system.

👤

Third-Party Services

LargeNote does not integrate with, or share data with, any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services. The only third party involved in your use of LargeNote is Apple, through the Mac App Store distribution and optional iCloud (if you choose to store files there via Finder — this is entirely outside LargeNote's control and governed by Apple's own privacy policy).

👶

Children's Privacy

Because LargeNote collects no data from any user, there is no special risk to children. The app is safe for users of all ages. We comply fully with COPPA and equivalent regulations by nature of our zero-data-collection design.

🌍

International Users & GDPR

Because we collect no personal data, we have no obligations under GDPR, CCPA, or similar frameworks regarding data transfer, deletion requests, or data processing agreements. There is simply no data to process. If you are located in the EU or California, you can use LargeNote with full confidence that your rights are inherently protected.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL. Any material change that affects data collection would only take effect in a future app version clearly described in the App Store release notes. Given that our core promise is zero data collection, any such change would be exceptional and prominently disclosed.

Questions about privacy?

We're happy to answer any questions you have about how LargeNote handles — or rather, doesn't handle — your data.
